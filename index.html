About the Founder

I am the founder of Lyceum Law Chambers, a Delhi-based legal practice where I combine rigorous legal analysis with hands-on financial expertise to deliver advice that is not only technically sound but also commercially sensible.

My work spans Accounting & Taxation (direct/indirect tax advisory, compliance, assessments, structuring), Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) (arbitration and mediation to resolve disputes faster and at lower cost), Corporate & Business Law (contract drafting/review, policies, regulatory compliance), and Civil & Matrimonial matters (property, family, and allied disputes).

What sets my practice apart is an outcome-driven approach - I start with your objective and work backwards laying out options, risks, timelines, and costs in clear terms. I value ethics, preparation, and service, and I prefer amicable, enforceable resolutions wherever possible. Where litigation becomes necessary, I proceed strategically and efficiently.

I also work in a hybrid model offering both online consultations and in-person conferences support is accessible and timely. If you're looking for straightforward guidance, carefully drafted documentation, or a decisive resolution pathway, Lyceum Law Chambers is built to help.
